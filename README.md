About the Project
WhatNext Vision Motors isn't just a project — it's a glimpse into what the future of the automobile industry could look like when technology, data, and human-centered design come together.
Built as part of the SkillWallet program using Salesforce Trailhead, this project gave us the opportunity to step into the shoes of a real automotive business and think deeply about what it takes to serve modern customers well. From managing leads to resolving service issues, we mapped out the full journey — and then built it.
---
What We Were Trying to Solve
The automotive space is evolving fast. Customers expect personalized experiences, instant responses, and smooth service — and businesses that can't keep up get left behind.
We asked ourselves: what would a future-ready car company actually look like under the hood?
That question became the foundation for everything we built. Our goal was to design a system that helps an automobile company:
Build genuine, lasting relationships with its customers
Keep sales teams organized and focused on what matters
Handle service and support without letting things fall through the cracks
Use real data to make smarter, faster decisions
---
Tools and Technologies
We worked primarily within the Salesforce ecosystem, using Trailhead as our learning and implementation platform. Here's what went into building this:
Salesforce Trailhead — our core platform for CRM development and learning
CRM concepts — the backbone of every feature we designed
Business process modeling — to map out workflows before building them
GitHub — for version control, collaboration, and presenting our work
---
What We Built
Customer Management System
We created a centralized place to store and manage everything about a customer — their history, preferences, and interactions. No more scattered spreadsheets or lost conversations.
Sales Pipeline Tracking
From the moment someone shows interest in a vehicle to the day they drive it home, every step is tracked. Leads, follow-ups, opportunities, and conversions — all in one view.
Service and Support Module
When something goes wrong, customers need help fast. This module handles incoming service requests, assigns them to the right people, and keeps customers updated throughout.
Reports and Dashboards
Numbers don't mean much until they tell a story. We built dashboards that surface the insights that matter most — helping managers make decisions based on evidence, not gut feeling.
---
Working as a Team
One of the best parts of this project was building it together.
We split responsibilities across modules, held each other accountable, and learned how to communicate clearly when things got complicated. It was a real simulation of how product teams actually work — with deadlines, dependencies, and the occasional disagreement that made the final result better.
