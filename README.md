# 🚗 WhatNext Vision Motors  
### *Shaping the Future of Mobility with Innovation and Excellence*

---

## 🌟 About the Project  
Welcome to **WhatNext Vision Motors**, a project focused on reimagining the future of mobility through innovation, smart technologies, and customer-centric solutions.  

This project was developed as part of a **SkillWallet / Salesforce Trailhead-based learning experience**, where we explored how modern businesses can leverage CRM platforms to enhance operations and customer engagement.

---

## 🎯 Objective  
The goal of this project is to design and demonstrate how a forward-thinking automobile company can:

- Improve customer relationship management  
- Streamline sales and service processes  
- Use data to make smarter decisions  
- Deliver a seamless customer experience  

---

## 🛠️ Technologies & Tools Used  

- ☁️ Salesforce Trailhead Platform  
- 📊 CRM (Customer Relationship Management) concepts  
- 🧠 Business process modeling  
- 📁 GitHub for version control & presentation  

---

## 🚀 Key Features  

- 🔹 **Customer Management System**  
  Efficiently manage customer data and interactions  

- 🔹 **Sales Pipeline Tracking**  
  Track leads, opportunities, and conversions  

- 🔹 **Service & Support Module**  
  Handle customer issues and service requests  

- 🔹 **Reports & Dashboards**  
  Visualize business insights using Salesforce analytics
